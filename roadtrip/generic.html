We are a Full House

10.05.2024

We are a vibrant household within the community of Sunshine Acres Children Home. A family of seven boys, a spirited girl thriving with husband and wife team that learns, grows, and shares life together. This dynamic family embraces a lifestyle rich in adventure and creativity, always wanting to embarking on an adventure, engaging in various sports, and exploring the beauty of national parks. Their shared love for the outdoors not only fosters bonding but also instills valuable life lessons about teamwork and resilience.

Through artistic endeavors, they encourage self-expression and creativity among the boys, creating a nurturing environment where each child can flourish. Central to their family values is the commitment to teaching the boys about Jesus, the importance of being responsible community members, and how to navigate in the modern technical world. By integrating faith into their daily lives, they provide a moral compass that guides the children as they navigate their formative years. Our AV family's approach emphasizes support, ensuring that each boy feels valued and understood. Together, they are not just a group of kids; they are a united team dedicated to building a brighter future for each child in their care.
